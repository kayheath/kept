# UX Decision Log — todo-app

Canonical record of design & experience decisions. Spines win on conflict with any mock, wireframe, or import.

| # | Date | Decision | Rationale / Source |
|---|------|----------|--------------------|
| 1 | 2026-06-15 | UX run started (Create mode, coaching path). Sources confirmed: product brief + brainstorming session. | Kayla |
| 2 | 2026-06-15 | Product name: **Kept**. A calming, offline, single-user todo app — an emotional-regulation tool disguised as a todo app. Built for one person (Kayla's partner). | brief + brainstorm |
| 3 | 2026-06-15 | **The Design Law (load-bearing):** "The love in Kept is never conditional, never withheld, never earned." Notes appear regardless of completion — especially on bad days. Presence is a constant, not a reward. Every feature/copy/interaction is checked against this. | brief + brainstorm |
| 4 | 2026-06-15 | Core inversion: **celebrate done, not pending** (build-up, not burndown). Done-pile / win-wall is the dominant view; pending is held quietly. | brief + brainstorm |
| 5 | 2026-06-15 | **Anti-patterns (hard nos):** no red, no badges, no counts, no streaks, no exclamation marks, no countdown pressure, no gamified urgency, no required fields on capture, no full-backlog view, no finish-to-unlock gating. | brief + brainstorm |
| 6 | 2026-06-15 | **Horizon Limit:** default horizon is today; even the gentle peek is scoped to today. The future stays in the vault. | brainstorm #11 |
| 7 | 2026-06-15 | **Home screen IA:** current task in focus → glanceable hint of next → growing done-pile (with partner's notes woven through) → always-available "show me my day" gesture (bucketed, today-scoped, opens calmly). Calm by default, control on demand. | brainstorm #4, #5, #17 |
| 8 | 2026-06-15 | **Frictionless capture:** type a few words, done. No required fields. App auto-sorts into a bucket. | brainstorm #15 |
| 9 | 2026-06-15 | **Two-date model:** optional hard **due date** (real deadline, held quietly as safety net) + optional **do-date** (when he intends to work on it; organizes the daily view). | brief + brainstorm #16 |
| 10 | 2026-06-15 | **Note vault:** partner pre-writes notes, saved on-device. Three rarities — everyday warmth / rare "blue-moon" surprises / anchor-specific (gym, hard work day). A timing engine reads the shape of his day and surfaces the right note. The vault is content + a first-class deliverable. | brief + brainstorm #6,#7,#8 |
| 11 | 2026-06-15 | **Tone shifts with the clock:** Loving Coach by Day → Soft Landing by Night (pressure dissolves into acceptance + fresh slate). Encouragement ≠ guilt. | brief + brainstorm #9 |
| 12 | 2026-06-15 | **Slow completion ritual:** deliberate gesture → task drifts softly into done-pile → partner's note blooms in. Completion is a moment, not a checkbox. | brief + brainstorm #19 |
| 13 | 2026-06-15 | **Empty-day invitation:** on a cleared day, gently offer an optional pull-ahead win — never pressure. **Day journal:** completed tasks + the note that appeared stack into a revisitable keepsake (separate, opt-in; home stays today-scoped). | brief + brainstorm #10,#18 |
| 14 | 2026-06-15 | **Technical floor:** fully offline / local-first; on-device storage; no accounts, cloud, sync, live connection, analytics, or tracking. Stack TBD (PRD/architecture). | brief + brainstorm |
| 15 | 2026-06-15 | **Form factor RESOLVED: desktop application.** Interaction language is click + keyboard, not touch/haptics. Completion ritual = click-and-drift animation (no haptic). Capture = keyboard-first (type + enter). "Show me my day" = click/keypress. Mobile gesture/haptic language from brainstorm is superseded. | Kayla |
| 16 | 2026-06-15 | **Visual mood: warm, dim, cozy. Dark theme** (warm dark, not cold/clinical). Aim for a low-light, exhale-when-you-open-it feeling. | Kayla |
| 17 | 2026-06-15 | **Plant motif:** partner loves plants → use **pixelated plant sprites** as a cozy, cute visual language (cozy-game warmth, e.g. Stardew-adjacent calm). Note: brief says structure stays grounded/clear; plants are the ambient warmth, not whimsy injected into task mechanics. | Kayla |
| 18 | 2026-06-15 | **Partner's voice is visually set apart** from the app's own text (distinct treatment — TBD exactly how: e.g. a softer/handwritten-feeling typeface vs. the app's main face). | Kayla |
| 19 | 2026-06-15 | **Plant motif = BOTH ambient + functional.** Ambient pixel plants warm the space, AND a hero plant/garden grows as he completes tasks — the done-pile rendered as a living, growing thing. "Win-wall" becomes a "win-garden." Completion ritual: task drifts away → growth happens (sprout/leaf/bloom) → partner's note blooms in. The build-up metaphor and the emotion do the same work. | Kayla |
| 20 | 2026-06-15 | **Import: `imports/plant-sprite-sheet.png`** — user-supplied pixel-art plant sprite sheet. Contains multi-frame bulb→sprout→stem→bloom GROWTH sequences (drives the done-pile growth animation), succulents/cacti/leafy greens, terracotta & clay pots, soft pink/yellow bloom accents (+ some reds to avoid). This is the source-of-truth sprite asset for the plant visuals. | Kayla |
| 21 | 2026-06-15 | **Palette direction: earthy green-forward, anchored on Slate & Sage** (user: "Slate & Sage is the most him," "he loves earthy green colors"). Terracotta/clay as warm earthy secondary (from pots); sage/leaf/succulent greens for growth; soft dusty-pink & pale-gold bloom accents for rare-note moments. No red. | Kayla |
| 22 | 2026-06-15 | **COLOR PALETTE LOCKED: "Candlelit Sage"** — base `#16191A`, growth/sage green `#9DBE9A`, terracotta/earth secondary `#AD6A38`, pale-gold bloom glow for completions, set-apart partner-voice tone (warm). | 23 | 2026-06-15 | **Typography direction:** App's own voice = clean, grounded, highly readable (soft humanist sans) — structure stays calm, no whimsy. K's note voice = **handwritten/script**, so her words literally feel like they're in her hand; this is where the personality lives. Two-voice system. Exact families chosen at Finalize. | Kayla |
| 24 | 2026-06-15 | **No notifications, ever. Kept "stays put."** Zero OS notifications/push. Nothing intrudes; everything (notes, day-coach encouragement, nudges) waits calmly inside the app and is discovered only when Christopher chooses to open it. Reinforces "suggests calm, never imposes." Day-coach tone therefore lives as in-app warmth, not push reminders. | Kayla |
| 25 | 2026-06-15 | **Protagonist name: Christopher.** App's own UI text stays generic (never addresses him by name). Personalization by name lives ONLY inside K's handwritten notes, where Kayla can include "Christopher" whenever she likes. | Kayla |
| 26 | 2026-06-15 | **No vault-authoring UI in scope.** Kayla seeds notes directly into the data layer (she expects something like Prisma + a local DB). The app only READS/surfaces notes; it does not provide an authoring/editing surface. Christopher's side is the only designed surface. (Implies a JS/TS-friendly local stack — e.g. Prisma + local SQLite — fits the "local-first, no server" floor; final stack TBD in architecture.) | Kayla |
| 27 | 2026-06-15 | **Vault is hidden from Christopher by design** (Design Law): he cannot browse the unsurfaced pool of pre-written notes — that would make the love feel scheduled/mechanical. He receives notes only as they bloom. NUANCE: notes ALREADY surfaced may reappear in the **Day Journal** keepsake (already "delivered," safe to revisit); only the unsurfaced future pool stays hidden. | Kayla |
| 28 | 2026-06-15 | **Note matching — control dials (per-note tags Kayla can set):** (1) **Time-of-day targeting** (morning/evening/night windows); (2) **Specific-date notes** (pinned to an exact day — exam, birthday, hard anniversary); (3) **Trigger moment** (first win of the day / finished everything / a day with nothing done / specific anchor task like gym completed); (4) **One-time vs. reusable** (show-once-ever vs. can-reappear). EXCLUDED: per-note rarity weighting — the three rarity *buckets* (everyday / blue-moon / anchor) stay as-is without numeric weights. These dials define the note data model + the timing engine's selection logic. | Kayla |
